# References

This repository includes third-party source snapshots and builds on several
research lines. Verify exact BibTeX metadata against the official paper pages
before paper submission.

## Core Gaussian Splatting

- 3D Gaussian Splatting for Real-Time Radiance Field Rendering.
- Street Gaussians for modeling dynamic urban scenes with Gaussian Splatting.
- Driving-scene Gaussian Splatting and autonomous-driving neural rendering
  methods used as comparison baselines.

## Geometry Priors

- Depth Anything and Depth Anything 3 for monocular / multi-frame depth priors.
- Monocular depth priors for 3D reconstruction and neural rendering.
- Boundary-aware geometry regularization and depth-edge consistency methods.

## Data and Evaluation

- Waymo Open Dataset for autonomous-driving street-scene data.
- Sparse LiDAR supervision and LiDAR-valid geometry evaluation.
- RGB, depth, and structure-aware evaluation metrics for novel-view synthesis
  and reconstruction quality.

## Gaussian Control and Repair

- Gaussian densification, pruning, opacity regularization, and opacity control
  methods.
- Counterfactual and influence-style attribution methods for rendering or
  differentiable graphics systems.
